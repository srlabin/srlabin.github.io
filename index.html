Genome Engineering and Stem Cell Laboratory

Sivaprakash Ramalingam Lab at CSIR-IGIB

Our laboratory is working in the area of targeted genome engineering and regenerative medicine with an ultimate goal of establishing tools for enabling cell and gene therapy. To accomplish this, we are adopting an interdisciplinary strategy to develop alternate therapy models, primarily for monogenic hematological diseases.
